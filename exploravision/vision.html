Our Vision

Our application will take notes from what it hears. By turning it on during a lecture or a important conversation will help you save important notes and understand conversations better. The way that this application is not one dimensional either as it could be used for any situation where notes or understand is important.

Our application uses a NLP to collect and translate data which is then sorted through a machine learning system. Once the system is sorted it will be altered into an easily understandable format that the user will be able to make sense of.
